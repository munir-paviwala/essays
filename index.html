Hello, I am Munir!

Let's unpack why I said that:

It takes a lot of 'freedom' to do art. Freedom To waste resources.

---

Very simply, some bahut purani (very old) societies were formed in order to get some freedom but kabhi toh you just couldn't - pregnancy, injury, caretaking, old age etc.ke liye (for which) you had to take time off from producing/foraging resources.

Resources - food, fuel, shelter and clothing - were necessary to live. Gathering these takes energy - a lot of which came from food itself, fire made it easier to access that food energy, and shelter and clothing helped survive longer - but took energy to make.

All this energy gives freedom to not die from hunger or environment. Some may call this earliest phase of 'history', atleast John Richard Hicks of A theory of Economic History (1969) would say this.[^1]
